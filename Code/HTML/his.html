History

Much has happened since cloud computing was thought up by Joseph Carl Robnett Licklider, a computer scientist. Since then, there have been leaps and bounds in the world of the cloud. There were still times when the idea for cloud computing and the innovations that came from it were forgotten, waiting for people to rediscover them. It just so happened that certain companies did- companies like Salesforce and Apple were working to revive cloud computing. Eventually, companies such as Amazon and Microsoft entered the market with products that gave business even more tools to more productivity and security.
