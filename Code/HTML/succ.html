Superiority

Bit Torrent is a system of computer-to-computer networks, and does not use a server for the information to be stored. Instead, the data is stored on your computer and different people in the BitTorrent network. The reason why Torrent networks are less common is because they are generally slower, and less broad. Torrent is also generally less secure than cloud computing, so this is why this is rarely used, and cloud computing is much more practical.
