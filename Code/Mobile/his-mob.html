Overview

Much has happened since cloud computing was thought up by Joseph Carl Robnett Licklider, a computer scientist. Since then, there have been leaps and bounds in the world of the cloud. There were still times, however, when the ideas and innovations computing cloud computing were forgotten, waiting for people to rediscover them. It just so happened that certain companies did - companies like Salesforce who were working to revive cloud computing. Eventually, companies such as Amazon and Microsoft entered the market with products that gave businesses even more tools to increase productivity and security. Today, the cloud market is an extremely valuable one that businesses rely on for success.
